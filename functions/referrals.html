Frequently Asked Questions
How do I refer a friend? +
Simply share your unique referral code. Friends enter it during registration.
When do I get referral commissions? +
You receive a 5% commission instantly when your referred friend makes an investment. Minimum investment for them is $110.
How do tier bonuses work? +
Tier bonuses are awarded when your total number of referred members reaches specific milestones: $100 for 11 members, $250 for 21 members, and $700 for 51 members. These are one-time bonuses per tier reached.
Is there a limit to referrals? +
No, refer as many friends as you like and keep earning!
